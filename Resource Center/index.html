Case Study

Executing heart failure care between visits

ISHI demonstrated that protocol-driven care can improve guideline-directed therapy, manage high volumes of alerts, and extend care without adding workload to community cardiologists.

Clinical Outcomes
15.5% Improvement in GDMT Utilization
99% Clinical Alerts Addressed Within 48 Hours
81% Alerts Managed by Nursing Team
Operational Scale
747 Heart failure patients managed across community cardiology practices.
2,013 Clinical alerts managed through structured workflows.
0 Escalations required to community cardiologists.

Based on published ISHI clinical outcomes and heart failure care program data.
